Job specifications

Requirements:

The HotBeansWeb team assists businesses, schools, and governments in making the transition to HotBeansWeb products and provides ongoing support. You listen to the customer and solve technical issues quickly to demonstrate how our products can make businesses more productive, collaborative, and innovative. 
You collaborate with a cross-functional team of web developers and system administrators, as well as a diverse range of regional and international customers. Your customer relationships are critical in assisting HotBeansWeb in growing its Cloud business and assisting companies around the world in innovating.

Minimum qualification in this areas:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Information Systems, related technical field, or equivalent practical experience.
  • Experience in reading, debugging, and writing code (e.g., Java, C, C++, .NET, Python, Shell, Perl, and JavaScript). Experience advocating for customers needs.

If you meet these requirements, please fill out the application form and email it to us at: s2000476@leyton.ac.uk
